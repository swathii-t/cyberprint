Our Mission

In a world where most of our time is spent on the digital web, we have to educate ourselves on how to utilize our vital digital resources in a safe yet fun way. Introducing CyberPrint, your online tool on how to navigate the vast internet. As a website visitor, you can learn about how to become digital aware while playing our trivia quiz, browsing through resources, reading about digital citizenship, and learning about the impacts of your digital footprint. Together, let’s surf the web while being internet safe and mindful of our online actions.
